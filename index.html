Keyboard Sortable Demo

To rearrange items using a keyboard: move focus to a site's drag handle, then use Ctrl + Up Arrow and Ctrl + Down Arrow shortcuts to change its position.
Alternatively, press Space to enter 'drag' mode and rearrange items with just the Up and Down arrow keys.
